This element is used to specify the geographic location that is the subject of resource content.  A discussion is necessary on how to deal with resource content that is related to geographically disconnected locations, e.g. 'Comparison of the Taupo, New Zealand and Geysers, CA geothermal systems'.  This would most accurately be a collection of bounding boxes or center points, suggesting that BB cardinality should be 1..* and center point cardinality 0..*.
