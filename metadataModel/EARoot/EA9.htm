Properties of an Agent; the name 'ResponsibleParty' is inherited from ISO19115. The general relationship between an agent and a resource is best represented by a Link, with identifiers for the Agent and the Resource, with the predicate linking them expressing the kind of relationship. The Metadata entity in this model hard codes sever key relationships--metadata contact, resource contact, author (intellectual originator), and distributor contact.
